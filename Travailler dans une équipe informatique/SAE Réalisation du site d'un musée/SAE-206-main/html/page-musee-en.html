General information about the music museum

History:

The museum of music of the philharmonie de paris located in the 19th arr. The building of the museum was the old conservatory of Paris built in 1795. The latter was renovated and transformed into a music museum in 1997. The museum is under the responsibility of the state.


Description of the museum :

The museum has a collection of more than 7000 instruments of which 1000 are exposed. It presents the evolution of the instruments and the music through the times and the cultures, so it proposes concerts of the temporary exhibitions and even activities for the children.


Visitors' opinion about the museum:

Visitors recommend this museum, which offers a rich and diverse cultural experience, with exhibits on the history of music and instruments of the world as well as a pleasant atmosphere. Prices are reasonable, with free admission for those under 25 and free audio guides available.
